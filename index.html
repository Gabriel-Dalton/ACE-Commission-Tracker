Commission Structure & Rules
Target
$1,000 in eligible new MRR per month. Resets each calendar month.
Above target
10% commission on the new MRR above the $1,000 line, calculated on first-year contract value (12 × monthly MRR), paid as the client pays.
Below target
Hourly rate only. No commission on Platform deals below the threshold.
Eligible plans
Scale, all Pro tiers, and all Unlimited Domain plans count toward target and earn commission.
Excluded plans
Lite, Starter, and Growth — self-serve tiers — do not count toward target and do not earn commission.
Managed deals
Flat $300 commission per deal, regardless of size. Compensates for dev hours, audit hours, and referral fees baked into Managed pricing.
12-month cap
Commission stops after 12 months of payments per client, or earlier if the client cancels.
Open question
Whether Managed deals count toward the $1,000 monthly target alongside the flat $300. Toggle below to model both scenarios.
